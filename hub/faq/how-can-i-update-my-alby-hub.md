# How can I update my Alby Hub?

It is your own Hub. You decide when to update it. To do so go to your Alby Account dashboard ([https://getalby.com/dashboard](https://getalby.com/dashboard)) or directly to [https://getalby.com/node](https://getalby.com/node) to update your Hub. &#x20;
