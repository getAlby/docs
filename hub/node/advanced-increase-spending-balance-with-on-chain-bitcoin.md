# 📤 Advanced: Increase Spending Balance with on-chain bitcoin

## Introduction

To increase your spending balance you need to either top up your balance ([guide](../wallet/buy-bitcoin.md)) or open a lightning payment channel using the bitcoin On-Chain Balance in your Hub. Opening a new channel helps you to accomplish the following:

* Transfer funds from your On-chain Balance to your Spending Balance
* Top up your Spending Balance
* Be ready to send bitcoin via the lightning network

\
**That’s it.** By opening a lightning channel with your On-Chain Balance your are connecting your Alby Hub to the lightning network and you are transferring sats to your Spending Balance. 🚀
